Exercise Rider

A rider built much like a jockey but is employed for exercising and working out horses in the morning.

Lasix

A medication for the treatment of bleeding in horses that is legal in North America, but not all worldwide jurisdictions.

Sloppy

A wet racetrack condition, typically with a firm base and water settling on top.
